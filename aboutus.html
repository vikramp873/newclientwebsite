Citizen Umbrellas Pvt.Ltd

We have achieved the root to tips of backward integration in such a way that we make high quality fabric from yarns, wide varieties of handle and umbrella parts from plastic granules, wire drawing for umbrella ribs and conversion of CRC sheet from high frequency welding machine to make umbrella shaft, all types of springs that are used in an umbrella and we even make the nails that are used in assembling, and for final packaging we have our own in-house corrugation unit to make corrugation boxes.

We have manufacturing units located in Kolkata in West Bengal, Surat and Umbergaon located in Gujrat and regional offices in Kolkata, Delhi and Mumbai. Citizen has a reputation for competitive pricing and impressive range of umbrellas. Our ultimate goal has been to provide the most cost effective products that too with good quality. We do so by ensuring that every umbrella we manufacture is durable, functional and value for money. We pride ourselves on living upto these standards. It is because our pursuit to relentlessly innovate. We are the only company in India to manufacture umbrella cloth. With new designs churning out every year in our fabrics and new and innovative moulds in our moulding division our customers are ensured of a varieties of novelty umbrellas every season.

Currently we are only catering to India, and hence we have always kept in mind the people in India. This is why we make our product affordable and at the same time quality efficient. we want to make sure that all the people are protected from any weather that they may come. Hence our slogan is “ Suraksha Har Mausam Se” .

Umbrella being the cheapest and most visible form of advertisement, have been used by various corporates for marketing purposes. We are the torch bearers in this field and help corporates to ensure that their brand is seen and used by the masses. Our motto is utmost customer satisfaction and value for money.
