Our Story

1882

Citizen commenced umbrella trading.

1976

Installed 2 units for umbrella manufacturing in Kolkata.

1980

Expanded to a total of 5 units in Kolkata.

1990

Added a new unit in Kolkata for umbrella and umbrella parts.

1994

Established regional offices in Mumbai & Kolkata.

1999

Initiated umbrella manufacturing operations in Umbergaon (Gujarat).

2001

Began injection moulding for all plastic parts used in umbrellas.

2003

Inaugurated our own corrugated box unit to meet in-house requirements.

2005

Upon realizing Citizen was a major consumer of umbrella fabric, we ventured into coated fabric manufacturing for umbrellas. Our totally integrated unit not only made fabric from yarn but also coated the same.

2012

Began supplying bag fabrics to industry giants such as VIP, Samsonite, and Wildcraft.

2016

Established a new unit in Umbergaon for luggage manufacturing.

2018

Owing our formidable growth to our large infrastructure, our monthly capacity increased from 10000 pieces to 300000 pieces in a span of one year.

2020

We are the leaders of the umbrella industry. In the fabric industry, we manufacture 35 million metres monthly. With 1000 sewing machines, we are one of the largest bag manufacturers in India.
